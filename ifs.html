IFS Visualiser

This visualiser starts with a simple seed shape and repeatedly replaces it by transformed copies of itself. Each step applies the listed maps to the previous set, so the construction stages show how \(E_1, E_2, E_3,\ldots\) converge toward the final self-similar fractal.

Deterministic IFS (mask iteration)

E(n+1) = union w_i(E(n)) using inverse-mapped pixels for crisp paper-like figures
