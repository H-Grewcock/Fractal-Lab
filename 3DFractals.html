3D Fractals Lab

The canvas shows projected points from a 3D fractal: points are generated in space, then drawn on the screen through the current camera angle. Escape-time examples keep points that survive an iteration rule, while the IFS tab repeatedly applies vertex moves or affine maps to build self-similar cloud. The dimension tools measure the original 3D point cloud using box-counting or correlation estimates.
